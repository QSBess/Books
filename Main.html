"If you don’t like to read, you haven’t found the right book." -J.K Rowling

In an ever-changing world, many things from the past can be forgotten.
Past novels from talent authors could be lost in the sea of stories that flow from every corner of the modern entertainment industry.
Technology is at the core of this industry boom, and can be used to drive out the past or preserve it.
Remembering Books is a tribute to past, strong female authors, who should be remembered for their great influence and enganging stories.
