Little Women- Louisa May Alcott

Book Summary

Little Women is a tale of four sisters, Jo, Meg, Beth, and Amy March, who live in New England around the time of the American Civil War. Each sister deals with their own struggles as they grow into women. The story centers around family, nineteenth century restraints on women, and class issues.
