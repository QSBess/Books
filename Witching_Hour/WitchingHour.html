Witching Hour- Anne Rice

Book Summary

The Witching Hour is the first novel in the series Lives of the Mayfair Witches. It begins the tale of a family of witches, and a phantom that has been the source of their fortunes for generations. The thirteenth witch in the family, Dr. Rowan Mayfair, and her new love, Michael Curry, work with an occult organization, called the Talamasca, to resist the spirit’s attempts to be born into flesh. However, Rowan is torn between her scientific curiosity, and her resolve to end the nightmare.
