Frankenstein- Mary Shelley

Book Summary

The tale of Frankenstein starts with the Captain Robert Walton writing letters to his sister. His crew rescues a nearly frozen and emaciated man named Victor Frankenstein on their journey to the North Pole. Frankenstein recounts a story of his life'(’)s miseries to Walton as a warning. His pursuit of science and fame had led him to create a monstrous but sapient creature in an unorthodox scientific experiment.
