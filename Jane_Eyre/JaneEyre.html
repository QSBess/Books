Jane Eyre- Charlotte Bronte

Book Summary

This novel is the story of Jane Eyre, an orphaned, isolated ten-year-old, living with a family that dislikes her. She grows, excels at school, becomes a governess, and falls in love with Edward Rochester. However, everything is not as it seems with Mr. Rochester, and Jane soon discovers the shameful truth. After being deceived by him, Jane goes to Marsh End, where she regains her spirituality, discovers her own strength, and finds in herself forgiveness and love for Mr. Rochester.
