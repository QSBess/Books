Pride and Prejudice- Jane Austen

Book Summary

Pride and Prejudice is a love story set in early nineteenth century England about two strong personalities who come from different societies. Jane is from a large modest family, with several sisters, an eccentric father, and an outrageous mother. She is strong, outspoken, and fun. Darcy is a wealthy, reserved, and stern man who is used to upper-class society. Jane's family goes through several trails, which but pressure on her to marry. Darcy initially seems like another obstacle for Jane, but eventually proves himself more than she first believed.
