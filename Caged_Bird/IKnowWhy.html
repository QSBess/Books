I Know Why The Caged Bird Sings- Maya Angelou

Book Summary

In I Know Why the Caged Bird Sings, Maya Angelou describes her coming of age as a bright but insecure black girl in the American South during the 1930s and subsequently in California during the 1940s. When she is only three years old, her parents divorce and ship Maya and her older brother, Bailey, to live with their paternal grandmother. Maya and Bailey struggle with the pain of having been rejected and abandoned by their parent.
